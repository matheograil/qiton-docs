## Why we started Qiton ?

Esports teams get access to hard-to-understand data under the format of JSON Lines (20,000+ lines each).
This ecosystem is still young and teams often don't have the budget to hire experienced developers to handle all of this data.
Our goal is to make this data more accessible and easier to understand for the coaches and analysts.
