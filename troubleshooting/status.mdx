## Monitoring

<Info>You could experience some latencies everyday at 3:00 AM UTC due to the daily backup process.
    Should not take more than 5 minutes.</Info>

We use Hyperping to monitor the status of our services. You can check it at any time by visiting [qiton.hyperping.app](https://qiton.hyperping.app).
